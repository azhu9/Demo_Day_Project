Recyclable Plastics

There are many examples of plastic that can be recycled such as water bottles, plastic trays, or shampoo bottles. The issue is that they are not taken care of properly as they are not recycled which cause them to become a threat to the environment. For example, many recyclable water bottles are left on the beach and eventually pollute the ocean and pose a threat to the animals in it. Beverage bottles are one of the 3 leading causes of pollution within the ocean. The only 2 numbers of plastic that can be recycled are 1, Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET), and 2, High Density Polyethylene (HDPE).
